Ingredients:
 - 1 tea-spoonful of Nescafe Classic
 - 1 tea-spoonful of sugar
 - 3-4 ice cubes
 - 1 straw

Directions:
 1. In a shaker, add 30 ml of water, the Nescafe and the sugar. Close
    the shaker and shake very well for about half a minute.
 
 2. Pour the foamy content of the shaker in a tall water glass.
 
 3. Add the ice cubes, fill the glass with water, insert the straw and
    stir for about 10 seconds.

 4. Enjoy!
